Cricket Minesweeper

Score: 0/0

Instructions:

  • Each box in the grid may have a fielder, runs to score, or a power-up.
  • Your goal is to avoid wickets and score the maximum amount of runs.
  • You have a total of 3 wickets to defend. If you lose all wickets, the game will be over.

Gameplay:

  • Left-click on a box to reveal its content.
  • If the box contains runs, your score will increase based on the number of runs (0 to 6).
  • If the box contains a power-up, a special effect will be triggered:
    • P
      Power-up 0 (Free Hit): Grants you a free hit. The next hit won't result in a wicket even if you hit a fielder.
    • P
      Power-up 1 (Double Hit): Doubles the score of your next hit. Make the most of it!
    • P
      Power-up 2 (Fielder View): Temporarily reveals the locations of fielders in the grid.

Tips:

  • Strategize your hits to avoid fielders and maximize your score.
  • Make use of power-ups wisely to gain an advantage.
  • Take note of the locations of fielders when the Fielder View power-up is triggered.

Enjoy playing Cricket Minesweeper!
